Privacy Policy

Sacred Calendars & Faith

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sacred Calendars & Faith. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your name and email address through Google Sign-In.
  • User Content: Comments and posts you share in the Community section.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app usage to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Enable community features and user interactions
  • Improve and personalize user experience
  • Send important updates about the app
  • Respond to support requests

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use local storage (AsyncStorage) for preferences and secure cloud storage for account data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With service providers who assist in app operations

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
